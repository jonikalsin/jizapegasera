Tamika Scott Bio (Wiki)

Who is Tamika Scott?

Tamika Scott is a recognized R&B singer, songwriter, and actress from America, who began her professional career in 1992.

Of all things, she is much more popular as a member of the multi-platinum-selling R&B group called Xscape.

Aside from that, Xscape reformed in 2017 and announced plans to make a biography about the band.

Scott released her first single after rejoining Xscape on March 25, 2019, titled “Tonite.” In August of the same year, she released “Almost Over,” another single.

Tamika Scott- Bio, Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

The American singer, Tamika Scott was born on November 19, in the year 1975, in College Park, Georgia, the United States.

She is 47 years old and of American nationality as of 2023. Similarly, her ethnicity is Caucasian and her birth sign is Scorpio.

Tamika Scott was born to Rev Randolf Scott, her father, and Scott McFarlin, her mother. She grew up with one sibling; LaTocha Scott.

Talking about her education history, she attended Tri-Cities performing arts high school in East Point, Georgia. Tamera Coggins was added as a fourth member, although she did not remain with the group for long.

Tamika Scott- Professional Life, Career

Tamika Scott began her music career with the formation of Xscape alongside her sisters and friend Kandi Burruss.

In 1993, the band released their debut album, “Hummin’ Comin‘ at ‘Cha,” which peaked at No. 17 on the US Billboard 200 list.

“Off the Hook,” their follow-up album, was even more successful, debuting at No. 5 on the US Billboard 200 list and gaining platinum certification from the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).

Tamika Scott pursued a solo career after the trio broke in 1998, and her debut album, “Tamika Scott,” was released in 2007.

Hit songs from the album were “Day and Night” and “Greatest Gift.” Scott has dabbled in acting, appearing in TV episodes and films such as “Martin,” “In the House,” and “House Party 3.”

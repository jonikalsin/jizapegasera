 A bright young model and competitor in beauty pageants, Sienna Weir had her sights set on the Miss Universe crown. Who is Sienna Weir?

On May 4, 2023, at the age of 23, Sienna Weir, a gifted and attractive model, equestrian, and finalist for Miss Universe Australia, passed tragically. On April 2, 2023, she passed away as a result of wounds she received in a horseback riding mishap at Windsor Polo Grounds.

She was taken by ambulance to Westmead Hospital, where she was kept alive for a number of weeks before her loved ones had to say farewell. Sienna developed an early interest in the world of fashion and beauty and had aspirations of being a model.

What is the Net Worth of Sienna Weir? Salary, Earnings

Where was Sienna Weir born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Sienna Weir was born in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia, on October 27, 1999, and spent most of her childhood there. Since she was three years old, she had a fascination for horses, and she participated in show jumping competitions all across the nation.

She also had a passion for English literature, particularly Gothic poetry, and she graduated from Sydney University with a double degree in English literature and psychology.

Sienna was renowned for her vivacity and outgoing nature. Weir was particularly close to her mother, who frequently accompanied her to events and photo shoots. Sienna had a tight relationship with her family.

Weir characterized herself as an adventurous, social, committed, multi-faceted, and integral individual who never shied away from trying something new or pursuing her aspirations. She intended to relocate to London in order to advance her professional life and spend more time with her sister, niece, and nephew.

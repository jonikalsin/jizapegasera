Nick Cannon Defends Lil Meech Amid Summer Walker Cheating Scandal

Nick Cannon has come to the defense of Lil Meech after the BMF actor was accused of cheating on his now-ex-girlfriend Summer Walker.

On Thursday (August 3), Cannon took to Instagram and posted a picture of him and Meech giving each other love during the latest episode of Wild ‘N Out.

“I believe you my young brother!” the actor/comedian said. “I’m always helping my cousins with the groceries! Such a F*ckin Gentleman!”

Meet Love Is Blind's Kyle Abrams! His Bio, Height, Family, Job, Age

Contestants on Love Is Blind Season 2 each had their own approach to finding love, and Kyle Abrams certainly was the one who’d punch through the wall!

Keep reading this Kyle Abrams Bio to learn more about him.

Love Is Blind 2: Are Shaina Hurley And Kyle Abrams Still Together?

Sadly no, Kyle Abrams and Shaina Hurley are not together. Well, considering their conflicting perspective about religion, and Kyle’s “necessary compromises,” it was only expected of the duo to fall apart.

Let’s take a look at what happened, from the very beginning.

Among the 15 females who competed on Netflix’s Love is Blind season 2, Kyle developed a strong connection with Shaina Hurley. The two bonded over being old souls with their mothers as their biggest influences, and also since Kyle’s profession was the same as that of Shaina’s parents, the two were able to find a common ground.

As the bond got deeper, Shaina didn’t mind even revealing that she’d been engaged once before, and it didn’t work out as her fiancee was “very controlling.” But instead of being put off, Kyle assured the lady before cheekily adding, “and then you met me.”

Though sure that the two wanted each other, Kyle and Shaina both then continued their exploration — with Shaina eyeing Shayne Jansen. However, Shayne had taken a liking to Natalie Lee. Even on the decision day, when Shaina confessed to Shayne, she had deep feelings for him, he shut her down!

Thus, Shaina zeroed out on Kyle.

However, Shaina was still happy to have Kyle as her partner. But again, while all was going well between the two, they realized one major problem — Shaina was a devout Christian, while Kyle was an atheist!

The disparities caused quite a conflict between the duo. But Kyle said that he was ready to make the necessary compromises, and proposed to Shaina.

Surprisingly, she said “Yes!”

Maybe the fact that Kyle gave Shaina his mother’s engagement ring to prove his sincerity, might have affected the lady’s judgment.

The pair slept in separate rooms on the very first night of their getaway, and eventually, Shaina decided that she would go back home  — to Chicago — all by herself.

Undeterred, Kyle still managed to reach out to her once he went back to Illinois. But Shaina just couldn’t make peace with the fact that Kyle was an atheist.

Kyle Abrams Age

Kyle Abrams was 29 years of age when he first appeared on Love is Blind in 2022.

He is 3 years younger than the then-32-year-old hairstylist, Shaina Hurley.

Trivia: All excited to appear on Love Is Blind, Kyle took it to his IG on Feb 2, 2022, to share, “Will this KYLE find love behind the wall or punch through it? Find out on @netflix @loveisblindnetflix February 11th!”

Kyle Abrams Job

Kyle Abrams is a Glazier.

What’s that? Well, Kyle’s job as a “glazier” asks him to be the lead for measuring, installing, and repairing glass in houses, hotels, shops, and offices. In simple words, Kyle was a glass expert.

Reportedly, a glazier in the US made around $40 thousand per annum.

When Kyle wasn’t working, he love spending time in the sea; especially jet skiing. But Kyle isn’t just your average jet skier — the guy can fly the boats 6 inches above the waves! Check this video.

How Tall Is Kyle Abrams?

Kyle Abrams stands tall at a height under 5 feet 10 inches (178 cm).

Kyle Abrams Family

Kyle Abrams comes from a family of four — he, his parents Michael and Gwen Abrams, and his sister Brittany Schiffley.

His dad, Michael (a Mount Prospect, Illinois, native) was the one who taught the star his Glazier skills. As of 2022, Michael also worked as a Glazier at “Glaziers Union Local #27 – Chicago.”

As for Kyle’s mother, Gwen is a Maine North High School 1978 graduate. Not much was known about her.

Talking about Kyle’s sibling, Brittany lived in Island Lake, Illinois with her husband  Jonathan Schiffley and their cute little daughter. Reptodlty, Brittany married her husband on November 10, 2018, at Mickey Finn’s Brewery.

Sadly, Kyle’s grandmother has long passed away. He once took it to his IG to post her photo captioned, “Grandma😞smarter and more beautiful than I’ll ever be.”

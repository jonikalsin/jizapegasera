Who is Wanda Jackson:

Wanda Jackson is a famous Rock Singer. She was born on October 20, 1937 and her birthplace is Maud, OK. Wanda is also well known as, First popular female rockabilly and rock ‘n’ roll singer who sang “Tears Will Be the Chaser for Your Wine” and “Fancy Satin Pillows.”

Wanda is originated from United States. She briefly dated Elvis Presley in 1955 while on tour with him.

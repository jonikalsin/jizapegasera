Pics Of The RHOA Star &amp; ANTM Winner Hollywood Life

Eva Marcille first rose to fame as a contestant on the third cycle of ‘America’s Next Top Model’ in 2004. She was known as ‘Eva Pigford’ on the show, and was just 19 years old at the time of the competition. Eva went onto win ‘ANTM.’ She was in school at Clark Atlanta University in Atlanta, Georgia, but she left school after being name the winnre of ‘ANTM.’ After receiving a modeling contract through her big win, Eva began posing for magazines and walking in runway shows. She left her post-‘ANTM’ management in 2006, and is now signed to L.A. Models. Since being on ‘ANTM,’ Eva has picked up some acting roles, and she started appearing on Bravo’s ‘The Real Housewives of Atlanta’ as a recurring character and friend of NeNe Leakes during season 10 in 2018. She was named a main cast member for the show’s 11th season, which premiered at the end of 2018. Eva got engaged to Michael Sterling in 2017, and they were married in October 2018.

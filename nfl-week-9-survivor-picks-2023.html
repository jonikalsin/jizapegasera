Dolphins vs. Chiefs

Opening Line: Chiefs -2.5 | Over/Under: 51.5

The Kansas City Chiefs are slight favorites in their home game against the Miami Dolphins, with an opening line of -2.5. This suggests a close contest, but with the high Over/Under of 51.5, a high-scoring game is anticipated.

Strategy:

  • Targets: The Chiefs’ explosive offense makes them a solid pick, especially if Patrick Mahomes is in top form.
  • Avoids: Be cautious about the Dolphins; while they have shown flashes of brilliance, their inconsistency could be a factor.

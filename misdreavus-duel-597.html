Misdreavus (Japanese: ムウマ Muma) is a figure in Pokémon Duel. It was made available in version 6.2.6.

Release information

This figure was available as an Achievement Reward during the October 18 to 23, 2018 Queen's Cup.

Trivia

  • Prior to version 7.0.14, this figure was unable to be purchased in the Material Exchange.

Origin

Infiltrator is an Ability in the Pokémon games, though Misdreavus cannot have it. Shadow Sneak, Curse, and Confuse Ray are all moves in the Pokémon games that Misdreavus can learn, Shadow Sneak and Curse via breeding. Never-Ending Nightmare is a Z-Move in the games that Misdreavus can use by equipping Ghostium Z.

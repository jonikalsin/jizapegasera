'On My Block' Season 4 Set to Premiere on Netflix This October - Get a First Look!

On My Block is coming back for it’s fourth and final season!

Netflix has released a first look at the new season of the show, which is set to debut on October 4, 2022.

Click inside to see the pics…

The coming of age comedy follows a group of bright and street-savvy friends navigating heir way through high-school in the inner city.

The fourth season will pick up two years after season three’s shocking time jump, which revealed each of the main characters had undergone a drastic change from their high school self.

On My Block stars Sierra Capri, Jason Genao, Brett Gray, Diego Tinoco, and Jessica Marie Garcia.

It was announced that the show was coming to an end in January. See what other shows have been cancelled at Netflix here.

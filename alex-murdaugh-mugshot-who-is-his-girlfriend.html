Who Is Buster Murdaugh Girlfriend?

Alex Murdaugh, a prominent American lawyer family member, has been indicted and accused of more than 100 offenses. He is also accused of killing his wife and son.

Murdaugh’s other accusations include drug, money laundering, and fraud. The trial for murdering his wife and son started on 25 January.

His surviving son, Buster Murdaugh, testified in his dad’s double murder trial Tuesday (21 February 2023). The trail is still ongoing.

In today’s article, let’s explore how Alex’s wife and son died and get to know Buster Murdaugh’s girlfriend.

Alex Murdaugh Mugshot

This week, the law enforcement unit detaining disgraced and barred lawyer Alex Murdaugh in the Lowcountry of South Carolina uploaded his new mugshot (feature image).

Murdaugh can be seen wearing what appears to be a white and brown gingham-style shirt with a white T-shirt underneath on the first day of testimony in his double homicide case.

The defendant’s initial mugshot was made public in late September 2021, following his arrest in connection with an alleged insurance fraud scheme that is thought to be only distantly related to his ongoing murder prosecution.

Murdaugh made another appearance in a mugshot in July 2022, wearing a short-cropped haircut and being heavily toned down. After the defendant showed up in court for a brief bond hearing where he agreed to spend the whole of his murder trial in prison, that picture surfaced.

Alex Murdaugh: Trial Evidence

Alex Murdaugh allegedly murdered his wife, Margaret “Maggie” Murdaugh, 52, and Paul Murdaugh, 22, in June 2021. After 19 months of speculation, prosecutors opened the double murder trial for the disgraced SC lawyer Wednesday.

They were found shot with at least one bullet to the head by two different firearms. Paul Murdaugh’s brain blasted from his skull as the shotgun was so close to his head and was discovered at his feet.

While his lawyer discussed the gruesome details at the trial, Alex Murdaugh cried.

As for the evidence against Alex, prosecutor Creighton Waters said in his opening statement that bullets were pulled from bodies that matched ammunition boxes from about the home, phones that were never used again, and a seat belt with gunshot residue.

For the last three weeks of the trial, prosecutors tried to overcome the absence of concrete evidence, such as an eyewitness, linking Murdaugh to the murders.

Who Is Buster Murdaugh Girlfriend Brooklynn White?

Murdaugh Murders: A Southern Scandal, the newest true crime docuseries on Netflix, chronicles what happened with the Murdaugh family during the historic murder trials.

We may not have seen much in Murdaugh Murders (docuseries), but Brooklynn White is a reliable and honest girlfriend of Buster Murdaugh. She has been supporting her beau throughout the Murdaugh trial and challenging times.

Buster has faced loss like no one else – his mother and brother were killed, possibly by his own Father. Also, one of his friends, Stephen Smith, was killed in 2015 – Smith was mysteriously found dead in the middle of the road, and his death was ruled a hit-and-run.

As reported by Distractify, Brooklynn White owns a condo in Hilton Head, South Carolina. She is also a Juris Doctor Candidate at the South Carolina School of Law. Brooklynn went to the University of Alabama and graduated with a degree in political science in 2018.

The 26-year-old lady and Buster might have crossed paths with their studies circle in law while working in different law firms. White, a Delta Zeta sorority member, volunteered with the Miracle League of Tuscaloosa and the Special Olympics.

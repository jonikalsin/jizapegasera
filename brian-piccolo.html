Biography

Brian Piccolo was born in Pittsfield, MA on October 31, 1943. Former NFL running back who died at the age of 26 with embryonal cell carcinoma; became the subject of the 1971 TV movie Brian’s Song. He was portrayed by Sean Maher in the Hollywood remake of Brian’s Song in 2001. He was named ACC Player of the Year in 1964, after an impressive season at Wake Forest University.

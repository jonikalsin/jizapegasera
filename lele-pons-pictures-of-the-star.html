Pictures Of The Star Hollywood Life

Lele Pons is an Internet celebrity who rose to stardom on the social media app, Vine. Of course, she was devastated when the app shut down in 2016, but she has continued to remain present in the online community. Now, Lele shares her content via YouTube, Instagram and Twitter, boasting millions of subscribers and followers on all of the platforms. In addition to producing content for the Internet, Lele has also dabbled in some music and acting. She’s made appearances on various television shows and in some movies, and was host of ‘La Voz Mexico’ in 2018. She’s also starred in music videos for artists like Camila Cabello, Blink 182 and more, while also releasing some of her own music. Lele has won awards at the Teen Choice Awards, Streamy Awards, and has been nominated at the iHeartRadio Music Awards. Keep clicking through the gallery for more photos of Lele.

Sinead Oconnor Career

Sinead Oconnor is a well-known Irish Singer, songwriter, musician, and former priest. She has released 10 studio albums: The Lion and the Cobra (1987), I Do Not Want What I Haven’t Got (1990), Am I Not Your Girl?

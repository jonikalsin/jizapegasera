Jodelle Ferland Biography

Jodelle Ferland is best known as Actress who has an estimated Net Worth of $4 Million. She played the role Bree Tanner in the Twilight Saga and appeared in Case 39. In 2006, she received widespread attention for her performance in the horror film Silent Hill. She was later cast in the television series Dark Matter. She was born on October 9,1994, Nanaimo, Canada. She provided voices for characters in the videogame BioShock 2. She began acting in commercials at the age of two and made her film debut when she was four. She costarred with Renee Zellweger in the 2009 horror film Case 39. Her parents are Valerie and Marc Ferland. Her sister, Marisha, is an actor and her brother, Jeremy, is a musician. Her zodiac sign is Libra

Who is Daniella Karagach Partner? Exciting Journey Of Pro Dance Champions

Professional dancer and choreographer Daniella Karagach can charm anyone with her dance skills and movements. Well, she is a three-time United States junior Latin champion.

Today, we will reveal the person who charmed this fantastic dancer. Yes, we are talking about Daniella Karagach partner. So, follow this article until the end to learn everything about him.

A Sneak Peek Into the Early Life of the American Dancer

  • Daniella was born in Brooklyn, New York, on December 26, 1992. Her full name is Daniella Brittany Karagach. Meanwhile, her parents are Mark Karagach and Regina Yvette Karagach, both immigrants from Moldova.
  • Mark and Regina separated just a year after Daniella’s birth. Therefore, she was raised by her single mother. At present, her current family includes step-father Arie Zurinam and step-sister Ariana Zurinam.
  • She grew up in the Bath Beach and Forest Hills neighborhoods of New York, and she developed a passion for dancing from a young age. Therefore, Daniella studied Latin and Ballroom dance at ages 6 and 11, respectively.
  • Karagach attended public school and completed her education at Brooklyn’s Franklin D. Roosevelt High School. Afterward, she continued her education at the Staten Island campus of the City University of New York and graduated in 2010.

Who Is Daniella Karagach Partner? Meet the Pro Dancing Couple

The American national has found tremendous success and popularity as a dancer and celebrity. But, her relationship journey is even more interesting than her career. She has already found the ‘one’ in the form of Pasha Pashkov.

Pasha and Daniella’s journey as a couple is nothing short of a romantic movie script. They met for the first time in 2009 as dance partners after being introduced by their coaches.

They instantly connected and soon turned their dancing partnership into a beautiful relationship. According to US magazine, Pasha became Daniella Karagach’s boyfriend in January 2010.

Similarly, after dating for around two years, the dancing couple became engaged in January 2012. Not long after, the betrothed couple tied the knot in front of their friends and family in 2014.

Daniella took on her husband’s name after the wedding and began using Daniella Karagach Pashkova as her official name. Over the years, the pro dance champions have participated in various competitions as partners and lovers.

What Do We Know About Pasha?

Pasha Pashkov was born on May 28, 1986, in Perm, Russia. However, he has not mentioned much about his early life. We do know that he has a brother.

Similarly, Daniella Karagach partner began dancing at the age of eleven. He studied at Baruch College and graduated with a Finance degree in 2008. He revealed that his ballroom teacher was the first to recognize his talent and asked him to dance.

Furthermore, despite this encouragement, he quit dancing after his first competition but returned after his mother’s friend asked him to be her daughter’s dancing partner.

After pairing up with future wife Daniella in 2009, the couple won multiple dance championships and represented the United States in the Ballroom World Championship and the World Cup.

However, the lovely couple battled against each other at the finals of the thirty-second season of Dancing with the Stars. They both lost the finals but showed unparalleled professionalism during the final.

Similarly, Daniella Karagach’s husband founded Wedding Dance School with his partner. The husband and wife duo teach betrothed couple’s dance moves for their wedding.

Does the Couple Have Any Children?

As of 2023, the dancing power couple has been married for nearly ten years. Therefore, fans and followers of this celebrity couple want to know whether they have entered parenthood.

Good news for Karagach fans: the lovely couple announced their pregnancy in November 2022 by sharing a picture of Daniella holding tiny shoes and mentioning the baby’s arrival date as May 2023.

Furthermore, Daniella Karagach partner revealed that he is going to be the father of a baby girl in December 2022. Finally, on May 30, 2023, their daughter, Nikita Sofia Pashkova, was born.

We wish the dancing pair all the best in their journey as father and mother. Similarly, we hope that their personal life will become as glamorous as their professional life.
